## 🔢 Questions & Solutions

### ✅ Q1. Merge Sorted Arrays & Middle Sum

📄 

**Problem:**
Merge two sorted arrays and find the sum of the two middle elements.

**Approach:**

* Use two-pointer technique to merge arrays
* Find middle indices after merging
* Compute sum of middle elements

**Example:**
Input:
`arr1 = [1, 3, 5]`
`arr2 = [2, 4, 6]`
Output: `7`
